View Tickets for Project

View Ticket Progress Screen

On this page, you will be able to view the progress tickets on the system.

Navigation process

 

The Client needs to login into the system. Once they login, they are redirected to the Client Home screen where any projects or complete details are displayed for their viewing. They then click on the Projects tab on the side navigation bar which will open the View Project Screen. The user then clicks on the View Progress tickets button on a specific project they wish to view. The system will then display the View Ticket Progress Screen which will display all the complete tickets for that project.
