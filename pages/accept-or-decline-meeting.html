Accept or Decline Meeting

Accept/ Decline meeting

On this page, you will be able to accept/decline meetings scheduled with yourself and clients.

Navigation process

The preloaded Sales Rep needs to login into the system. Once they login, they will be directed to the Sales Home page. Here they will click on the ‘My schedule’ Button on the sidenav as seen below.

 

 

Once the view schedule screen is loaded select the meeting block as shown below.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

View meeting details page

             

Reference Number

Control Name

Functionality Description

1

Labels

These labels display information regarding the meeting including date, name, agenda, location, client and client status.

2

Attendees labels

These labels display all the attendees with their relevant statuses.

3

Accept Meeting Button

When selecting this button, the logged in sales rep’s status for the meeting will be changed to accepted.

4

Decline Meeting Button

When selecting this button, the logged in sales rep’s status for the meeting will be changed to declined.

5

Back button

When selecting this button, the system will navigate to the view schedule page.
