Create Vehicle

Create Vehicle

On this page, you will be able to create a vehicle on the system.

Navigation process

The Admin will need to login into the system. Once they login, they will need to click on the Vehicle Management Tab in the Nav Bar. A new side menu will load. The admin must then press the Vehicle Button in the sidenav. Once the page loads, they must press the Add Vehicle Button
