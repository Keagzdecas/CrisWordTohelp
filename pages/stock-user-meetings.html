Stock User Meetings

Meeting Accept/Decline

On this page, the stock user will be able to navigate to the meetings onto the system. They will then be able to view, accept or decline a scheduled meeting

Navigation process

The preloaded Stock user needs to login into the system. Once they login, they will be directed to the products page. Here they will click on the ‘Meetings’ Button as seen below.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Then the stock user will select the meeting that appears on the calendar.
