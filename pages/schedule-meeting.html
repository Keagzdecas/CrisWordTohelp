Schedule Meeting

Propose meeting time slot

On this page, you will be able to schedule a meeting with the respective client and chosen attendees.

Navigation process

The preloaded Sales Rep needs to login into the system. Once they login, they will be directed to the Sales Home page. Here they will click on the ‘Schedule meeting’ Button on the chosen project as seen below.

Detailed Description of the page

Reference Number

Control Name

Functionality Description

1

Meeting Name textbox

Sales reps should enter the necessary Meeting Name into this textbox.

2

Agenda textbox

Sales reps should enter the necessary Agenda into this textbox.

3

Location textbox

Sales reps should enter the necessary Location into this textbox.

4

Time Slot dropdown

Sales reps should select the necessary time slot in this dropdown.

5

Meeting Date datepicker

Sales reps should enter the necessary meeting date into this datepicker.

6

Back button

When selecting this button, the system will navigate to the sales rep homepage.

7

Submit button

The submit button will be clickable once all necessary inputs have been done. When selecting this button, a new meeting will be saved to the system and all participating people will be emailed with the details.

8

Pre-selected sales rep

The sales rep that is logged in will be preselected on the system

9

Checkbox picker

Sales reps should select the necessary attendees here.
