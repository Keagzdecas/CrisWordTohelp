Sales Representative View Project

View Request for Quote – Sales Representative

On this page, the sales representative can view all of the request for quotes sent by clients.

Navigation process

The Sales Representative will need to login into the system. Once they login, they will need to click on the Expand button on the Sales Representative Homepage.
