Lodge Complaints

Lodge Complaint Screen

On this page, you will be able to lodge a complaint that is linked to a project on the system.

Navigation process

The Client needs to login into the system. Once they login, they are redirected to the Client Home screen where any projects or complete details are displayed for their viewing. They then click on the Projects tab on the side navigation bar which will open the View project Screen. The user then clicks on the lodge complaint button on the project they wish to place a complaint on. They then enter their complaint and click on the submit button where the project will then be submitted and sent to the project manager.
