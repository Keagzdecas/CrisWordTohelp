Reschedule Meeting

5.24 Reschedule schedule

On this page, you will be able to reschedule meetings with yourself and clients.

Navigation process

The preloaded Sales Rep needs to login into the system. Once they login, they will be directed to the Sales Home page. Here they will click on the ‘My schedule’ Button on the sidenav as seen below.

 

 

You would then click on the meeting block as seen below

 

You would then click on the reschedule button as seen below.

 

Reschedule page layout

Detailed Description of the page

Reference Number

Control Name

Functionality Description

1

Meeting ID label

This block displays The meeting name and time. The colour will change between green for accepted, red for declined and yellow for pending.

2

Labels

Labels that display details relevant to the meeting such as the agenda, date and time slot.

3

Meeting Date datepicker

The sales rep must input the updated meeting date here.

4

Time slot dropdown

The sales rep must select the updated time slot here.

5

Back button

When this button is selected the system redirects you to the view schedule page.

6

Reschedule button

This button will be clickable once all details have been entered. Once this button is clicked the meeting will be updated on the system and emails will be sent out to all the attendees.
