View Quotes

On this page, you will be able to register as a client onto the system.

Navigation process

The Client needs to login into the system. Once they login, they are redirected to the Client Home screen where any projects or complete details are displayed for their viewing. They then click on the Quotes tab on the side navigation bar which will open the View Quote Screen.
